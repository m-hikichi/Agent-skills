# Northstar brand mini-guide

- Primary: `#143B5D`; signal: `#F28C52`; canvas: `#F5F1E8`; ink: `#17212B`
- Use one signal color for emphasis, not decoration
- Logo clear space: at least the height of the `N` around all sides
- Never recolor, stretch, rotate, or place the logo on a busy image
- Photography treatment: warm neutral, natural collaboration, no staged handshake imagery
- Typeface fallback: `Noto Sans CJK JP`, sans-serif
